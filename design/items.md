# Items

## Weapon

- Damage type
  - Physical
  - Magical
    - Fire
      - Damage over time
    - Ice
      - Slow movement
- Weapon type
  - Use
  - Projectile
  - Damage type
    - Physical
      - Arrow
      - Bomb
    - Magical
      - Fire
- Damage modifier
- Range modifier
- Attack-speed modifier
- Special
  - Stun

## Chest

- Armor type
  - Physical
  - Magical
- Armor modifier
- Special
  - Reflect Projectiles

## Feet

- Movement modifier
- Special
  - Charge
  - Jump into enemies
  - Disengage (Jump) if enemy is close

## Head

- Unit amount modifier
- Damage modifier
- Special
  - Revive

# Rarity

- Common

  - 1 base modifier
  - 1 positive modifier
  - 2 negative modifier

- Uncommon

  - 1 base modifier
  - 1 positive modifier
  - 1 negative modifier

- Rare

  - 1 base modifier
  - 1 positive modifier
  - 1 negative modifier
  - 1 special

- Epic

  - 1 base modifier
  - 2 positive modifier
  - 1 negative modifier
  - 1 special

- Legendary
  - 1 base modifier
  - 2 positive modifier
  - 1 negative modifier
  - 2 special

# Game Start

Starting unit: Each player chooses between 3 items within each type (Weapon, Chest, Feet, Head).
